About Me

Hello! My name is Alex Le and I am an incoming software engineer at Amazon. I am curently a senior at Brown University, where I am studying Computational Biology with an emphasis on software engineering, deep learning, and molecular biology. My interests include software design and development, artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. I am passionate about using technology to solve problems and make a positive impact in the world. I am excited to bring my skills and interests to my career at Amazon and continue learning and growing as a software engineer.

Navigating My Portfolio

Portfolio Design

This website was inspired by the UI/UX of MacBooks. The navigation of this portfolio is similar to how one would use the desktop on a MacBook. You can interact with the navigation bar at the top, the dock at the bottom, and folders on the right.

  • Navbar: Information about current state of your computer
  • Dock: Information about work and education experience
  • Folder: Information about personal projects and interests

Curser Indicators

As you hover over different elements on the website, you may notice the cursor changing shape. This helps to provide visual cues for users as they interact with the website.

  • Arrow Curser: Indicates mouse location and indicates non interactive elements
  • Pointer Curser: Indicates a clickable element
  • Move Curser: Indicates a movable element
  • Down Right Curser: Indicates an expandable element
