Navigating My Portfolio

Portfolio Design

This website was inspired by the UI/UX of MacBooks. The navigation of this portfolio is similar to how one would use the desktop on a MacBook. You can interact with the navigation bar at the top, the dock at the bottom, and folders on the right.

  • Navbar: Information about current state of your computer
  • Dock: Information about work and education experience
  • Folder: Information about personal projects and interests

Curser Indicators

As you hover over different elements on the website, you may notice the cursor changing shape. This helps to provide visual cues for users as they interact with the website. Have fun exploring!

  • Arrow Curser: Indicates mouse location and indicates non interactive elements
  • Pointer Curser: Indicates a clickable element
  • Move Curser: Indicates a movable element
  • Down Right Curser: Indicates an expandable element

*Hint: Try hovering over different elements and observe which curser appears

  • Try clicking the elements at the top and bottom of the app window (ie: titlebar, close, minimize, expand, resize)
  • Try clicking elements in the toolbar and apps on the desktop/dock
